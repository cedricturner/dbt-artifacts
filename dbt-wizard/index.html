dbt's AI agent

dbt Wizard, explained

TL;DR

dbt Wizard is dbt's AI agent that knows your project natively: its models, tests, lineage, and metrics. So it answers from your certified definitions instead of guessing. And it does not stop at the answer. You give it intent, and it does the work and verifies it before you ever see it.

Think of your dbt project as a library. An AI can read every book on every shelf faster than any person ever could. But reading fast was never the hard part.

The hard part is trust and action: knowing which of two disagreeing books is right, and then actually doing something with the answer. Having every shelf in reach does not tell you which one holds the truth, and it never does the work for you.

dbt Wizard is the librarian. It knows the catalog, checks its own answers at the reference desk, and acts on what you ask: from question and answer, to intent and action.

Now the library has a card catalog. Every book gets a card with its subject, its shelf, and what it references, so the agent skips the wandering and answers faster. But a catalog only points.
